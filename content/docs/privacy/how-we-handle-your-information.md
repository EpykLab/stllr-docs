title: How we handle your information
---

At stellarbridge, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy
explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information
when you use our file transfer service.

### 1. Information We Collect

#### 1.1 Information You Provide

* Account information (name, email address, password)
* Billing information (processed through Stripe)
* Files and data you upload
* Communication preferences
* Support communications

#### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

* Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
* Usage data (access times, file transfer statistics)
* Performance data and error logs
* Cookie and similar tracking technologies data

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

* Providing and maintaining our service
* Processing your transactions
* Sending service notifications
* Improving our service
* Responding to your requests and support needs
* Detecting and preventing fraud or abuse
* Complying with legal obligations

### 3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. This includes:

* Encryption of data in transit and at rest
* Regular security assessments and audits
* Access controls and authentication mechanisms
* Physical security measures for our infrastructure
* Employee training on security and privacy practices

### 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

* Service providers (e.g., hosting, payment processing)
* Law enforcement when legally required
* Business partners with your consent

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

### 5. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. These include:

* Essential cookies for service functionality
* Analytics cookies to understand usage patterns
* Preference cookies to remember your settings

### 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

* Access your personal information
* Correct inaccurate data
* Request deletion of your data
* Opt-out of marketing communications
* Export your data
* Object to certain data processing

### 7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your information unless required to retain it by law.

### 8. International Data Transfers

We may transfer your data to servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for these transfers through standard contractual clauses and similar legal mechanisms.

### 9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect or maintain information from persons under 18 years of age.

### 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
