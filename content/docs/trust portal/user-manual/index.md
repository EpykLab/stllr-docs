# stellarbridge Trust Portal User Manual

### User sign-up

Fill out the form with your email address, phone number, and password. Click the "Sign Up" button to create your account.

  <img src="/docs/trust-portal/user-manual/user-sign-up.png" alt="user-sign-up" width="300">

### MFA setup

Follow the instructions to set up multi-factor authentication (MFA)
for your account. Scan the QR code using your favorite authenticator
app. You can also type in the secret key manually. You will be provided with a verification code. Enter the code and click "Verify" to complete the setup.

<img src="/docs/trust-portal/user-manual/user-mfa-setup.png" alt="user-mfa-setup" width="300">

### User login

Enter your email address and password. Click the "Login" button to access your account.

<img src="/docs/trust-portal/user-manual/user-login.png" alt="user-login" width="300">
    
### Trust Portal home

If you have already made requests for information, you will see them listed here at the trust portal home.

<img src="/docs/trust-portal/user-manual/user-requests-made.png" alt="Trust-portal-home" width="900">

### Make a request for information

Click on the "New Request" button to start a new request. Fill out the
form with the details of your request and click "Submit". You can
prepare a document with questions and upload it via "Choose File";
click "Submit Request" and the system will generate an answer. Your
answer will be reviewed by a team member and you will be notified when
it is complete.

<img src="/docs/trust-portal/user-manual/user-new-request-questionnaire.png" alt="User-new-request-questionnaire" width="500">

### View or download your requests

On the request page, use the "Actions" dropdown to download a package
of documents prepared for you or to "View Details" about your request.

<img src="/docs/trust-portal/user-manual/user-req-dropdown-for-details.png" alt="user-req-dropdown-for-details" width="900">

### The trust report

You will see the report on this page if you have selected "view Details". 

<img src="/docs/trust-portal/user-manual/user-request-detailed-report-top.png" alt="user-request-detailed-report-top" width="900">

You can also download the report at the bottom of the page.

<img src="/docs/trust-portal/user-manual/user-request-detailed-report-bottom.png" alt="user-request-detailed-report-bottom" width="900">
